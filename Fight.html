Fights

Sonny Liston I (February 25, 1964)

This fight, in which Ali won the World Heavyweight Championship for the first time, is perhaps his most famous. Ali predicted he would "float like a butterfly and sting like a bee," and he did just that, outmaneuvering Liston and winning the fight in the seventh round.

Sonny Liston II (May 25, 1965)

This rematch was even more controversial than the first fight, with Liston going down in the first round in what some believe was a "phantom punch" from Ali.

Watch Fight

Joe Frazier I (March 8, 1971)

This was the first of three epic fights between Ali and Frazier, with Frazier winning by unanimous decision. Ali suffered his first professional loss in this fight.

Watch Fight

George Foreman (October 30, 1974)

In what was dubbed "The Rumble in the Jungle," Ali used his "rope-a-dope" strategy, allowing Foreman to tire himself out before knocking him out in the eighth round.

Watch Fight

Joe Frazier III (October 1, 1975)

In the "Thrilla in Manila," Ali and Frazier fought for 14 brutal rounds in intense heat. Ali won by TKO when Frazier's trainer threw in the towel.

Watch Fight

Henry Cooper I (June 18, 1963)

In this fight, Ali (then still known as Cassius Clay) was knocked down by Cooper in the fourth round, but managed to recover and win by TKO in the fifth round.

Watch Fight

Floyd Patterson II (November 22, 1965)

In their second fight, Ali taunted and humiliated Patterson before knocking him out in the 12th round.

Watch Fight

Ken Norton I (March 31, 1973)

Norton broke Ali's jaw in the early rounds of this fight, but Ali managed to finish the 12 rounds. Norton won by split decision, but Ali would go on to win their next two fights.

Watch Fight

Leon Spinks I (February 15, 1978)

Spinks, an Olympic gold medalist with only seven professional fights under his belt, shocked the world by defeating Ali in a split decision to win the heavyweight title. Ali would win a rematch with Spinks later that year to become the first three-time heavyweight champion in boxing history.
